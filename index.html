Repairs & Support
Need computer help?

Is your computer slow or broken?

We can help. We have the tools, the minds and the motivation to get your computer fixed in no time. We will come to you and try and diagnose the problem on the spot (and potentially fix it too), otherwise if it's a big problem we'll take it in and let you know as soon as possible when you find out. Our prices are affordable for everyone and we never rip our customers off.

Or do you just need a little bit of help?

Our Remote Support service is perfect for you. If you're at home struggling with something on your computer and you need urgent help then please gives us a call and we can help you without even needing to leave our desks.

With a remote client, we can connect to your computer and fix the problem from our seats. This will mean us using your mouse while you guide us with your problem (don't worry, it's totally safe).

Contact us today and we'll get back to you.
